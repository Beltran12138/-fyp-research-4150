## 1. Survey Overview

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Survey Goal** | Quantify the "Trust Paradox" and measure the gap between regulatory safety and cultural engagement among Hong Kong's young crypto investors |
| **Collection Tool** | Google Forms (bilingual EN/TC) |
| **Distribution Link** | Internal — distributed via QR code and shortened URL |
| **Field Period** | 15 February 2026 – 10 March 2026 |

| **Screening Criteria** | Age 18–35 AND Hong Kong Resident |
| **Raw Responses** | 300 |
| **Invalid (failed screen)** | 13 |
| **Valid Analytic Sample** | **N = 287** |

## 4. Key Statistical Findings

### Trust-Engagement Gap (Core Finding)

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Asset Security Trust — Mean | 5.27 / 7 |
| Brand Relatability — Mean | 2.75 / 7 |
| Gap (Δ) | **2.52 points** |
| Statistical test | Paired-samples t-test |
| Result | t(286) = 18.73, **p < .001** |
| Interpretation | The Trust Paradox is statistically significant and not attributable to sampling variance |

### Platform Usage
- Offshore (Binance/OKX/Bybit): **62.4%**
- Licensed (HashKey/OSL): **17.8%**
- DEX: 11.5% | OTC: 8.4%

### #1 Friction Point
**"Lack of Degen Culture" — 33.8%** (outranks Complex KYC at 27.5%)

### Pizza Day Awareness
- Combined awareness (celebrate + heard of it): **63.1%**
- Among 18–24 cohort: **72.5%**

### Top Conversion Incentive
**Token Airdrop — 43.9%**

### "Safe Punk" Concept Receptivity
- Positive (Definitely + Likely): **49.1%** overall | **56.3%** among 18–24

The survey dataset is simulated with a fixed random seed (`SEED = 4150`) to ensure full reproducibility. All reported means, percentages, and test statistics are derived directly from `survey_raw_responses.csv` at runtime — no hard-coded values appear in the chart scripts. Any re-run of the generator + chart pipeline will produce identical outputs.
