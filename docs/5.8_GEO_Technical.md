## 5.8.3 Quick Answer Content Blocks

Three structured content blocks are published on hashkey.com/hkx and the RIB microsite, formatted for direct extraction by AI answer engines. Format follows the ≤40-word direct answer + source attribution pattern that Perplexity and ChatGPT preferentially surface (Frase, 2025).
