## COMM4150 FYP: Re-coding Trust | ZHAO Han (1155191400)

---

## Concept

18.1% of surveyed respondents prefer a physical pizza party as their primary campaign incentive (Section 2.2.1). Cautious Explorers (Segment B) over-index on this preference — a real-world event provides the tangible, community-affirming experience that converts consideration into commitment in a way no airdrop mechanic alone can achieve.

The strategic logic is spatial: holding a "degen" activation event in Central — Hong Kong's institutional financial core — is itself a Safe Punk provocation. The 10,001st Slice lands where the first 10,000 never did: inside the city's regulatory architecture.

**Date:** 22 May 2026 (Bitcoin Pizza Day, 16th anniversary)  
**Location:** Central, Hong Kong (IFC / Exchange Square precinct)  
**Duration:** 12:00 – 20:00 HKT  
**Target throughput:** 2,000+ attendees; VIP tier for existing HashKey verified users

---

## 5.5.1 Spatial Concept — "The Neon Registry"

The event takes the campaign's visual identity off-screen and into physical space. Design language: Neon Registry aesthetic applied to street-level activations — dark structural elements with HK neon palette (#FF2D78 / #00D4FF / #FF4500) on concrete and steel.

**Core installations:**

| Element | Function | Safe Punk logic |
|---|---|---|
| Pizza truck fleet (×2) | Food + visual anchor | Laszlo Hanyecz had Papa John's; we have a licensed exchange |
| Neon "10,001" installation | Photo op + social anchor | The number is the campaign — make it physical |
| Registration kiosks (×6) | On-the-spot HashKey KYC registration | Lowest-friction conversion: phone in hand, pizza in the other |
| Laszlo Bot terminals (×3) | Interactive bot demo + Pizza Persona quiz | Drives digital engagement at physical touchpoint |
| Airdrop countdown display | BTC price trigger countdown | Creates shared cultural moment at 20:10 HKT |
| 0xU founding wall | Attendees scan SBT QR + add name | Physical instantiation of the on-chain community |

**Application for this campaign:**

1. **Internal:** Spatial planning — test layout, flow, and kiosk placement before physical build

## 5.5.4 Content Capture Plan

The event is a UGC engine, not just a gathering. Every element is designed to produce shareable content:

| Capture moment | Format | Channel |
|---|---|---|
| Neon "10,001" installation | Photo | Twitter/X, Instagram, LIHKG |
| Pizza Persona quiz result cards | Screenshot | Telegram stickers, Twitter/X |
| Airdrop countdown + trigger | Video (15s) | Twitter/X, Telegram |
| 0xU founding wall scan | Photo | Twitter/X |
| Pizza + registration kiosk combo | Photo | LIHKG ("yes I actually did it") |

**LIHKG strategy:** Do not post brand content to LIHKG. Let attendees post organically. The 0xU founding wall creates a natural "I was there" narrative that LIHKG users will share without prompting — authenticity cannot be purchased, only designed for.

---

## 5.5.5 VIP Layer (Segment C — Compliance Converts)

Existing verified HashKey users receive:
- Priority entry (no queue)
- Exclusive floor access (separated VIP zone near countdown display)
- "Founding Member" SBT delivered automatically at event entry scan
- Early airdrop confirmation at 19:55 (5 minutes before public trigger)

The VIP layer transforms existing users from passive attendees into visible community leaders at the moment of maximum cultural amplitude.
